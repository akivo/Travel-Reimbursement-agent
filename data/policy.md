# HCL Corp — Employee Travel Reimbursement Policy
**Version:** 2.1 | **Effective:** Jan 1, 2025

## §1. General Rules
- Claims must be submitted within **30 days** of travel end date. Late = auto-rejected.
- Receipts required for any single expense **above $25**.
- Alcohol, personal shopping, entertainment are NOT reimbursable.

## §2. Flight / Air Travel
- Economy class only. Domestic max: **$800**. International max: **$2,000**.
- Business class only with VP pre-approval for flights over 8 hours.
- Receipt + boarding pass required.

## §3. Hotel / Accommodation
- Max **$200 per night** (taxes included).
- Hotel invoice required for all stays.

## §4. Meals & Per Diem
- Flat **$75 per day** per diem covers all meals.
- Alcohol explicitly excluded — will be deducted.
- Client entertainment meals capped at **$150/person** with separate approval.

## §5. Ground Transportation
- Taxi/Rideshare: max **$50 per trip**.
- Rental car: max **$80 per day**.
- Parking: max **$30 per day**.
- Personal vehicle: **$0.67 per mile**.

## §6. Approval Thresholds
- Up to **$200**: Auto-approved if compliant.
- **$201–$2,000**: Manager approval required.
- **Above $2,000**: VP approval required.
- Any policy exception → VP approval regardless of amount.

## §7. Incomplete Claims
- Missing receipt for item >$25 → that item rejected.
- If >50% of claim value undocumented → entire claim goes to Manual Review.

## §8. Duplicate Claims
- Same employee + same dates + same category = duplicate → auto-rejected.

## §9. Non-Reimbursable
Alcohol, personal shopping, gifts, fines, streaming, spa, family travel.
