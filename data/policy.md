# HCL Tech — Employee Travel Reimbursement Policy
**Version:** 2.1 | **Effective:** Jan 1, 2025

## §1. General Rules
- Claims must be submitted within **30 days** of travel end date. Late submissions are auto-rejected.
- Receipts required for any single expense **above ₹500**.
- Alcohol, personal shopping, and personal entertainment are NOT reimbursable.

## §2. Flight / Air Travel
- Economy class only. Domestic max: **₹15,000**. International max: **₹1,50,000**.
- Business class only with VP pre-approval for flights over 8 hours.
- Receipt + boarding pass required for all air travel claims.

## §3. Hotel / Accommodation
- Max **₹8,000 per night** (taxes included).
- Hotel invoice required for all stays. Homestays require a signed receipt.

## §4. Meals & Per Diem
- Flat **₹1,500 per day** per diem covers all meals.
- Alcohol explicitly excluded — will be deducted from any bill.
- Client entertainment meals capped at **₹3,000 per person** with separate approval.

## §5. Ground Transportation
- Taxi/Rideshare: max **₹1,500 per trip**.
- Rental car: max **₹3,500 per day**.
- Parking: max **₹500 per day**.
- Personal vehicle: **₹14 per km**.

## §6. Approval Thresholds
- Up to **₹10,000**: Auto-approved if compliant.
- **₹10,001–₹1,00,000**: Direct Manager approval required.
- **Above ₹1,00,000**: Vice President approval required.
- Any policy exception requires VP approval regardless of amount.

## §7. Incomplete Claims
- Missing receipt for item above ₹500 → that item is rejected.
- If more than 50% of claim value is undocumented → entire claim goes to Manual Review.

## §8. Duplicate Claims
- Same employee + same travel dates + same category = duplicate → auto-rejected.

## §9. Non-Reimbursable
Alcohol, personal shopping, gifts, fines, streaming subscriptions, spa/wellness, family travel.
